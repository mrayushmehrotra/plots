Guidelines for filling the Application Form

  • All information to be filled legibly.
  • Applicant(s) to sign on all pages of the Application Form. In case of joint applicants, signatures of all the applicants are required.
  • All documents need to be self-attested by the Applicant(s).

Documents to be submitted along with the Application Form

  • Copy of PAN Card / Form - 60 duly filled.
  • Copy of latest Aadhaar Card.
  • One recent passport size color photograph of each Applicant(s).
  • For companies, copy of the Memorandum of Association and the Board Resolution authorizing the signatory.
  • For Partnership Firms and HUFs, appropriate documents as per guidelines.
  • For NRI/PIO applicants, passport copies and bank details.
  • Photocopy of Photo ID proof issued by Central/State Authority (if PAN copy is not supplied).
  • Address Proof (Electricity Bill, Telephone Bill, Aadhar, Voter ID, or Passport).
